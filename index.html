About Fruits!

fruit

Fruit is a healthy and tasty part of our daily meals. It grows on trees, bushes, and vines, and comes in many colors and shapes. Apples are crunchy, bananas are soft and sweet, and oranges have a juicy bite. Eating fruit gives your body vitamins and energy. You can enjoy it fresh, in a smoothie, or sliced up as a snack. It's nature’s candy—good for you and super yummy!
